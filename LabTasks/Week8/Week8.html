2.3 Analysis

An advantage that a time-frequency analysis has over a waveform-based analysis is that when a song is converted into a time-frequency, you are able to see features in a much more distinctive manner than you are with a waveform-based analysis. Certain features, such as new instruments or changes in harmonies, can be seen more clearly within a spectrogram than a waveform. For example, on the song 'Fugue7', the moments within the song that feature vocals are more distinctively visible within the spectrogram than the other waveform.
